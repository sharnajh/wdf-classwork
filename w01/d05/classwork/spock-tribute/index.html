Leonard Nimoy

as Spock

1931 - 2015

Leonard Nimoy was an actor, author, director, singer, and photograher. His most well-known role was that of Spock in the Star Treck franchise. This character is a half-vulcan half-human star fleet officer. Together with James Kirk and the crew of the USS Enterprise, they explore the far reaches of space. Leonard portrayed Spock from 1964, in Star Trek: The Original Series to 2013 in the recent reboot film, Star Trek Into Darkness.
